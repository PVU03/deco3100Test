Indoor Environment Reports

Global Temperature


Global Temperture (TG) means the environment temperature due to factors such as air temperature, radiant heat and air movement

Predicted Mean Value


Predicted Mean Value (PMV) is a scale from -3 to 3 to predict how an occupant would feel when in an environment ranging from cold (-3) to hot (3)

Predicted Percentage Dissatisfied


Predicted Percentage Dissatisfied (PPD) means an estimated percentage of occupants who would be dissatisfied due to the thermal environment
